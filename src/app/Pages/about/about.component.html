Ojasvi — Meaning & Essence

Ojasvi DermaCare — where radiance meets rejuvenation.

Rooted in Sanskrit, Ojasvi means “one who is full of vitality and brilliance.” Our skin and hair care products are thoughtfully crafted to nourish, protect, and enhance your natural glow.

In Ayurveda, Ojas is the vital essence of health and well-being — a subtle energy that sustains life and radiates outward as luminosity and strength. At Ojasvi, we blend the wisdom of nature with the precision of science to restore balance, build resilience, and reveal your inner brilliance.

Story about the founder

Lakshmi had always been surrounded by nature’s gentle embrace. Growing up in southern India, she watched her mother prepare traditional body care remedies with meticulous care—nourishing hair oils, cooling kajal, soothing herbal pastes. These simple, homemade creations carried the wisdom of generations, and the love woven into them left an imprint on Lakshmi’s heart.

But as she grew older, her skin and hair began to reject the commercial products that filled store shelves. Harsh chemicals left her feeling irritated, disconnected from the harmony she once knew. Determined to find a solution, Lakshmi embarked on a deeply personal journey—crafting formulations that her body could accept, ones that wouldn’t cause discomfort, but instead, nurtured and healed.

Her journey led her to cultivate a garden—a living sanctuary filled with wildflowers, roses, hibiscus, and fragrant botanicals. As the seasons changed, so did her understanding of nature’s gifts. Each bloom carried its own essence, its own secret potential, and Lakshmi learned to harness their power in her skincare and haircare creations. Over time, her recipes evolved, blending Ayurvedic traditions with ancient Western herbal knowledge, weaving together wisdom from different corners of the world.

But her transformation went beyond the bottles and balms she crafted. The garden became a reflection of her own life, a space where she grew not just flowers, but food—fruits and vegetables that nourished her family, strengthening the connection between the land and the way she lived. In sharing these handmade products and the lifestyle she had embraced, Lakshmi found something even deeper: joy. It was in the quiet moments spent tending to the plants, in the laughter of loved ones who delighted in her creations, in the knowledge that she was walking a path shaped by intention and care.

Yet, her journey wasn’t just one of tradition and intuition—it was grounded in science as well. With a PhD in Neuroscience and years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, Lakshmi understood the intricate balance between research and natural healing. Her expertise allowed her to refine her craft, bridging the gap between ancient wisdom and modern knowledge.

Through it all, Lakshmi remained devoted to what she loved—her garden, her creations, and the gentle wisdom of nature. And in this journey, she found something truly priceless: a life filled with purpose, love, and the quiet power of healing.
